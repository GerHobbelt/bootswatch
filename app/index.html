Easy to Install

Simply download a CSS file and replace the one in Bootstrap. No messing around with hex values.

Open Source

Bootstrap themes are released under the MIT License and maintained by the community onGitHub.

Tuned for 3.3.1

Themes are built for the latest version of Bootstrap.Version 2.3.2andothersare also available to download.

Modular

Changes are contained in just two LESS or SASS files, enabling modification and ensuring forward compatibility.

Get Plugged In

AnAPIis available for integrating with your platform. In use byNodeBB,BootSnap, and others.
